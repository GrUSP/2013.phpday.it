Bio

Software engineer, working with PHP since 2005 and now CTO at Ideato. I have wide interests in several aspects of software development like automation, quality and testing. I'm co-author of "PHP Best Practices", published by FAG in 2012. In my spare time I love swimming, reading mangas and relaxing on the beach ;-)
